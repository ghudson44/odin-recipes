Protein Cake

Cake of sweet custard containing eggs and spam.

For those who want the best of both dinner and dessert. When you just need something salty and sweet. Or you're too lazy to cook 2 separate dishes. This creative selection will not disappoint!

Designed by world famous chef "Gordon Nasty". Guaranteed to get your taste buds turning! How can you resist this scrumptious looking dinnert?

The Stuff

  • Eggs
  • Spam
  • Instant Custard
  • Salt

How

  1. Boil eggs.
  2. Add eggs, salt, custard, and spam to pudding dish.
  3. Bake at 375 degrees: For 1 hour.
