Sausage O's

Vienna Sausage + Spaghetti O's.

For those who can't decide between dinner and dessert. When you just need something salty and sweet. Or you're too lazy to cook 2 separate dishes. This creative selection will not disappoint! Designed by world famous chef "Gordon Nasty". Guaranteed to get your taste buds turning! How can you resist this scrumptious looking dinnert?

  • Eggs
  • Spam
  • Instant Custard
  • Salt
  1. Boil eggs.
  2. Add eggs, salt, custard, and spam to pudding dish.
  3. Bake at 375 degrees: For 1 hour.
