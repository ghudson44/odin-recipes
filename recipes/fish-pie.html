Fish Pie

Pie containing the fish your uncle caught the other day.

Can't decide between apple and cherry pie for your families get together? Compromise! Thick, flaky crust of pie; with the warm and comforting stench of fish.

You can use up any left over fish and fish heads from the last fishing trip. Your guests will fall in love with the aesthetic. Easy as pie!

The Stuff

  • Pie Crust
  • Sugar
  • Fish
  • Salt
  • Nutmeg

How

  1. Season fish to your liking.
  2. Assort fish equally within pie crust.
  3. Combine: sugar, salt, nutmeg and sprinkle on pie.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees: For 1 hour.
